Wearable device for Mobility Analysis

Enhancing Smart Footwear with MEMS-Based Pressure Sensors and IMU Technology

Motivation

• Some patients often suffer mobility challenges and athletes strive for peak performance.

• Our aim is to design wearable footwear to track performance metrics and detect health anomalies for athletes and patients.

• MEMS based Pressure sensor and Inertial Measurement Unit is used to monitor the required data and assist in gait analysis.

• The product can provide actionable insights to optimize training, aid in medical recovery and enhanced comfort for users.
